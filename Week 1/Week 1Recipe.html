HOW TO MAKE A MRBEAST BURGER

YOU WILL NEED :

  • 1.Ketchup
  • 2.Mayonaisse
  • 3.Pickles
  • 4.Diced White Onion
  • 5.American Cheese
  • 6.Smash Burger Patty
  • 7.Soft Buns
  • 8. Mustard
    •

    STEPS

    1. Into a saucepan 1/4 cup of water, 1/4 whole milk and 2 tbsp breadflour to make tangzong
    2. Over medium heat mix until clumping and turn off and continue mixing.
    3. Into a mixing bowl, add half cup of lukewarm milk and 2.5 tsp of instant yeast and rest for 10 mins
    4. Add the tangzong and 2tsp of sugar and 1tsp milk powder and one whole egg and mix.
    5. Once mixed add 3 cups of breadflour and a pinch of salt.
    6. Add one tsp of warm butter and cover for 1.5 hr.
    7. Using wagyu, grind to mince meat.
    8. Add flour and put the dough on it.
    9. Press the dough into any breadshape you prefer into a bun shape and cover to rest.
    10. Add an eggwash and into the oven at 375 farhrenheit for 20 minutes.
    11. Chop the onions and picles.
    12. Take a ball of the wagyu and press it down and add seasoning of choice.
    13. Add a slice or two of America cheese and cover and cook until golden brown.
    14. Slice the bun and add mustard, mayo, chopped white onion, the patties, pickles, ketchup and the bun.
    15. Enjoy you MrBeast Burger.
